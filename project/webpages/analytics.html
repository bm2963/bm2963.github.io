What Leaders Can Monitor

Engagement Trends

Track registrations, attendance, repeat participation, and drop-off points over time to improve programming.

Event Performance

Compare events by turnout, satisfaction, and follow-on outcomes (internships, mentorship signups, competition entries).

Competition Outcomes

Review team performance, scoring distributions, rubric consistency, and published results across competitions.

Sponsor Engagement

Track sponsor requests, participation frequency, event involvement, and engagement pipeline status.

Student Development

Monitor resume upload rates, mentorship activity, and involvement milestones to understand career readiness.

Operational Health

Identify bottlenecks (manual steps, approvals, delays) and highlight areas where automation improves efficiency.

Example Insights & Alerts

CMIS Connect can surface actionable insights automatically, such as:

  • Attendance risk: events trending below expected registration-to-attendance conversion.
  • Engagement gaps: students registered but not participating after initial onboarding.
  • Sponsor pipeline: pending sponsor requests with no follow-up after 7+ days.
  • Competition fairness: large scoring variance between judges indicating rubric calibration needs.
  • Mentorship health: matches created but no meetings logged after 30 days.
