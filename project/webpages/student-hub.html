About CMIS Connect

CMIS Connect is a conceptual platform designed to unify CMIS programs into one streamlined digital experience. It supports students, faculty, sponsors, and alumni with a centralized space for engagement, operations, and long-term community growth.

Our Vision & Goal

Vision

To unify all CMIS programs into one intuitive digital platform that streamlines student engagement, enhances faculty operations, strengthens industry partnerships, and empowers alumni involvement.

Goal

Improve the CMIS community experience by providing one platform that supports students, faculty, sponsors, and alumni — while reducing manual administrative work and improving accuracy across operations.

Why It Matters

CMIS activities often live across multiple tools and manual processes. CMIS Connect brings everything together so opportunities are easier to find, workflows are more efficient, and engagement becomes measurable and sustainable.

Who It Serves

Students

Register for events, track involvement, build a professional profile, upload resumes, and discover opportunities.

Faculty

Manage competitions, logistics, communication, scoring workflows, and publishing results — all in one place.

Industry Partners

Engage with opt-in student talent, request involvement, participate in events, and support CMIS initiatives.

Alumni

Reconnect through mentorship, give back through involvement, and help students grow through real-world guidance.

What this website represents

This website is a prototype that demonstrates what CMIS Connect could look like as a unified platform. Each page represents a major capability area of the product.

  • Student Engagement Hub
  • Case Competition Management
  • Sponsor Access Portal
