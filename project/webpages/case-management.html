Core Faculty Capabilities

Competition Setup

Create competition events, define rules, add timelines, attach case packets, and publish requirements to students.

Team & Roster Management

Manage teams, student rosters, roles, and participation status. Track who is eligible and confirm registrations.

Digital Submissions

Collect slide decks, reports, and supporting files. Automatically timestamp submissions and enforce due dates.

Rubrics Builder

Build standardized rubrics by category, weight, and scoring scale. Reuse templates across semesters.

Judge Assignment

Assign judges to teams, avoid conflicts of interest, and provide judging instructions and scoring access.

Scoring & Results

Aggregate scores automatically, generate rankings, and publish results. Keep an audit trail for transparency.

Competition Workflow

CMIS Connect supports a simple, repeatable process for each competition:

  1. Faculty creates a competition (deadlines, rules, deliverables, rubric template).
  2. Students register and form teams (or faculty assigns teams as needed).
  3. Teams submit deliverables (slides/reports) through the platform.
  4. Faculty assigns judges and shares scoring access + instructions.
  5. Judges score using rubrics (standardized and weighted scoring).
  6. Results are published to students and sponsors (as allowed).
  7. Analytics update automatically for outcomes, participation, and engagement metrics.
