Welcome to CMIS Connect

CMIS Events & Engagement

Vision

Unify all CMIS programs into one intuitive digital platform that streamlines engagement, enhances faculty operations, strengthens industry partnerships, and empowers alumni.

Goal

Improve the CMIS community experience by providing a single platform for students, faculty, sponsors, and alumni while reducing administrative overhead.

For Students

Access events, competitions, mentorship, and career development tools from one dashboard.

For Faculty

Manage competitions, communication, and logistics through efficient digital workflows.

For Industry Partners

Securely engage with CMIS programs and identify student talent through a centralized portal.

For Alumni

Participate in mentorship and engagement opportunities that strengthen the CMIS network.
